📌 Overview

Your Smart AI Chatbot is a no-code educational chatbot built using Botpress. It teaches beginners foundational AI concepts—Machine Learning, NLP, Deep Learning, and AI Ethics—through interactive dialogues, Q&A sessions, and multimedia.

🌟 Features
No-code implementation with Botpress Studio
15 curated Q&A pairs on core AI concepts
Two structured conversation flows: NLP & AI Ethics
Multimedia support: diagrams and visual learning
Intelligent follow-up handling and learning recommendations
🧠 Sample Q&A
Q: What is AI?
A: Simulation of human intelligence in machines that can think, learn, and make decisions.
Q: What is Machine Learning?
A: A subset of AI where systems learn from data without being explicitly programmed.
Q: What is Natural Language Processing (NLP)?
A: AI field enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.
Q: What are AI Ethics?
A: Concerns around fairness, bias, privacy, accountability, and safe use of AI.
